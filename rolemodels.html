Kane Caples Capels is a nonbinary skater from Riverside, CA who challenges society's gender norms. They are also a huge advocate for Black Lives Matter and are constanly fighting for what is right.
"Self-expression is more important than ever because of the chain reaction it can start. It’s important to express yourself in order to show others that they can express themselves freely without worrying about other individuals’ negative opinions. A lot of people emphasize “representation” in their community because it helps inspire others to do the same." -Caples, interview with Flaunt
Ruth Bader Ginsburg RBG served as an associate justice of the Supreme Court of the United States from 1993 until she sadly passed on September 18, 2020. Throughout her life she advocated for Women's Rights, Reproductive Rights, and was a role model for women all across the globe.
"I said on the equality side of it, that it is essential to a woman's equality with man that she be the decision-maker, that her choice be controlling." -RBG
